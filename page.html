Description

Yoga, the meaning of which is union, is the deep scientific practice which has been studied and implemented in Bharat since time immemorial and is essentially the practice of being one with nature, the experience of going beyond the conception of the body and rather identifying oneself as a part of the cosmic whole, and in its highest form, making a connection with the Supreme sentient being, who is possessed of eternality, knowledge and bliss and realizing oneâ€™s relation with that Supreme Lord. The Yoga tours, taking you to the banks of the holy river ganges, who is the most purifying of all rivers, the forests in the Himalayas, where the sages in the bygone times have achieved this union with the Supreme, Vrindavan, the eternal residence of that very Lord, and many other such places, which will instill within the hearts of the seekers of the true essence of life a deep awakening and a feel of the experience of achieving the state of Yoga. The Yoga classes that will be a part of these tours will help in the understanding and experiencing of this great System of Union with the divine that has been practiced in India since ancient times.
